# Continuous Tenses
**be + -ing forma**

---

## Present Continuous

### Teigiamoji forma
- I am working  
- you are working  
- he is working  
- she is working  
- it is working  
- we are working  
- you are working  
- they are working  

### Neigiamoji forma
- I am not working  
- you are not working  
- he is not working  
- she is not working  
- it is not working  
- we are not working  
- you are not working  
- they are not working  

### Klausiamoji forma
- am I working?  
- are you working?  
- is he working?  
- is she working?  
- is it working?  
- are we working?  
- are you working?  
- are they working?
